SKIN CARE TREATMENT




CLASSIC FACIAL
A thorough skin analysis and in depth view into your lifestyle, genes and daily routine to come up with classic glow treatment plan. Our facial include deep cleansing, steaming followed by an exfoliation. This facial include 15 minute mask that is suited best for your skin needs, then serums, moisturiser and spf.

ACNE FACIAL
This facial is an alternative treatment designed to treat the root cause of the acne with minimal side effects. It starts with double cleansing, steaming then extraction and exfoliating. A peel or mask is applied, followed by AHA or BHA toner. Serums with high frequency to kill Acne causing bacteria, then moisturiser and spf.

DERMAPLANE FACIAL
this treatment begins with double cleansing, jojoba or organ oil is applied, then dermaplane begins (removal of vellus hair and dead skin cells), the face is cleaned with warm towel, followed with toner, then Serums, jade roller, moisturiser and spf. This service is to be repeated every four to six weeks. This treatment instantly reveals smoother and cleaner skin suited for all skin types, dermaplaning is manual, chemical free treatment that immediately improves skin tone, clarity and texture.

MICRODERMABROSION FACIAL
Is a Non invasive chemical free cosmetic procedure used to remove the top layer of dry dead skin cells to reveal healthy young looking skin and improve over all skin tone and texture. We start by deep cleansing, then use a diamond tip to slough off two to three weeks of dead skin, warm towel is then used to clean face, A hydrating mask follows, ice globe to sooth the face, some serums, moisturisers and spf.

KING FACIAL
This luxurious treatment is perfect for men, Purifying facial includes deep cleansing, dermaplane, steam, extended extraction, a tailored mask to your skin needs, high frequency, ice glosses to cool the skin. The experience ends with a relaxing head, neck and shoulder massage. After the facial our king’s skin will look luminous, lifted and smooth.

KYMI DELUXE FACIAL
Our signature glow facial gives you the red carpet glow that all celebrities have. This facial includes: double cleanse, steam, exfoliation and toner. We examine the skin to see if you need any extraction and perform if necessary. A light peel or enzyme peel is then applied followed by serums, jade roller, moisturiser, LED therapy and spf.

CHEMICAL GLOW PEEL
Is a medical grade technique used to improve the appearance of the skin. It is designed for skin rejuvenation of all skin types, addresses skin hyperpigmentation, sun damage, oily skin. This peel gently and effectively exfoliate while simultaneously hydrating and brightening the skin resulting in a fresh dewy glow from within.

Cellulite Reduction (60 minutes)
Cellulite is a term for the formation of lumps and dimples in the skin. Between 80 and 90 percent of women will probably experience cellulite. We utilise FDA approved method to stimulate collagen and breaking up the underlying fat accumulation. It eliminates toxins, improves lymphatic drainage, reduce swelling and cellulite appearance and smooth skin is on the way.

MICRONEEDLING
60 minutes (Vampire Facial) Rejuvenate your face with a micro needling facial treatment using micro channel tool to create tiny channel in the epidermis which has been proven to increase serum absorption. This is one of celebrity's known skin secret called; the Vampire Facial. Controlled injury treatment that uses fine needles to puncture the skin with PRP or growth factors to rejuvenate the skin (don’t worry your face would be numbed). It treats and improves conditions like Acne, Acne scarring, fine lines and wrinkles. This typically is performed in a series of four to six sessions spaced about a month apart.

LED PHOTOTHERAPY (45 minutes)
This treatment is the secret to a beautiful youthful skin, it is used to fix skin problems such as Acne. It also help improve the appearance of the skin revealing a noticeably firmer, smoother texture.

HYDRAFACIAL (60 minutes)
It a non-Invasive resurfacing facial that will enhance the health and appearance of the skin. It uses patented technology starting with cleansing by removing the dead skin cells and excess sebum from the skin. The skin is then infused with highly effective serum to plump nourish and hydrate the skin.

KYMI BACIAL (60 minutes)
Specifically created for the back, this treatment consists of deep pore cleansing, exfoliation, steam, signature mask, toning, serums then finish with moisturiser.
